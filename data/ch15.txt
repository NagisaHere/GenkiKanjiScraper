What is the difference between と思います と思っています	The first implies the decision is made in the moment The second implies the decision was already made						
what does -ておく describe?	action performed in preparation for something. e.g. 明日は試験があるので、今夜勉強しておきます Because there is an exam tomorrow, I will study (for it) tonight.						
What are noun qualifier sentences?	"known as a big noun phrase takes the form of ""qualifier sentence + noun"" used to add characteristics to nouns (e.g. its location, adjectives, verbs) e.g. 昨日買った + 本 (the book I bought yesterday)"
